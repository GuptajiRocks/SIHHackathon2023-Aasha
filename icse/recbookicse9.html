Science

  • When it comes to preparing effectively for the exam, textbooks are the best source of material to study. The ICSE Physics Class 9 textbook for ICSE covers all the topics prescribed in the ICSE Class 9 Physics syllabus. Students should have a thorough knowledge of the Physics textbook so that they can attempt the final question paper with full confidence.
  • CSE Physics Class 9 is a vast subject, and the set of questions asked in the board exams is also quite difficult when compared to the other boards in the country. Textbooks for ICSE class 9 have always been the storehouse of knowledge, and students should refer to these books for a better understanding of each topic. The chapters that are covered in the ICSE Class 9 Physics books are Measurement and Experimentation, Motion in One Dimension, Laws of Motion, Fluids, Heat, Light, Sound, Electricity, Magnetism, etc. While preparing for the exams, students should always practise numericals which are given at the end of each chapter and related questions which are given in different reference books, it would help them to score better marks in the board exams.
